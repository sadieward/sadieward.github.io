Impact of Immigration

This chart shows the immigrants coming into the UK are more likely to be young, working age individuals, with most of the distribution under the age of 45. This suggests immigration improves the age dependency ratio and has positive fiscal effects on the economy

A major concern of the public is migrants arriving in the UK with little willingness to work, however this chart shows the unemployment for the UK-born and EU-born are very similar. It is slightly higher for non-EU born migrants; however, they have converged slightly over the last 2 decades, with the unemployment rate for non-EU born migrants only being 2 percentage points higher in 2022.
