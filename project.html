Immigration in the UK economy

Public opinion on immigration is divided in the UK, with many misconceptions on the impact on GDP, employment and productivity. It was a major concern for the public voting in the Brexit Referendum, while research shows it has overall positive effects of the economy. How is immigration impacting the UK economy

The UK has experienced a steady stream of net migration of the last 2 decades. Most of the foreign born population is focussed in urban areas, particularly London.
